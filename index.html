OUR STORY

Luntra was born out of frustration. After trying to learn languages like French and Spanish using existing apps, I Ishimwe Eric realized that most tools focused heavily on grammar and vocabulary, but rarely helped with speaking. Despite my dedication, I saw little to no improvement in real conversations. That’s when it clicked: speaking is the heart of communication, and it deserves to be at the center of language learning.

WHAT WE DO

At Luntra, we help you master new languages by speaking not memorizing. Our platform connects you with an AI-powered conversation partner and real native speakers to practice real-life dialogue, improve pronunciation, and gain fluency fast. We combine smart technology with real conversation so you learn naturally, like a child learns their first language through speaking and listening, not just typing.

Got Questions?

What is Luntra?

Luntra is an AI-powered language learning platform that helps users master languages by practicing real conversations through speaking, not just memorizing vocabulary or grammar rules.

How is Luntra different from other language apps?

Luntra focuses on speaking and listening using an AI Talkbot that mimics real-life conversation. It's designed to help you gain fluency faster by actually talking, not just tapping.

How do I interact with real people on Luntra?

Luntra connects you with other learners based on language fluency. You practice speaking with real users who are fluent in the language you’re learning and they learn from you in return.

What languages can I learn on Luntra?

Luntra supports 100+ languages thanks to its advanced Talkbot. Whether it's Spanish, Korean, French, or Kinyarwanda—you’ll find your language here.
