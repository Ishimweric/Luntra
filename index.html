OUR STORY

Luntra was born out of frustration. After trying to learn languages like French and Spanish using existing apps, I Ishimwe Eric realized that most tools focused heavily on grammar and vocabulary, but rarely helped with speaking. Despite my dedication, I saw little to no improvement in real conversations. That’s when it clicked: speaking is the heart of communication, and it deserves to be at the center of language learning.

WHAT WE DO

At Luntra, we help you master new languages by speaking not memorizing. Our platform connects you with an AI-powered conversation partner and real native speakers to practice real-life dialogue, improve pronunciation, and gain fluency fast. We combine smart technology with real conversation so you learn naturally, like a child learns their first language through speaking and listening, not just typing.

Luntra Talkbot

What it does

It simulates natural conversations with an intelligent chatbot that listens, responds, and adapts based on the learner’s level and goals.


Impact

Builds confidence in speaking, improves pronunciation, and accelerates fluency through active speaking not just reading or writing.


Key features

  • Real-time spoken interaction
  • Language-level personalization
  • Feedback on grammar, pronunciation, and vocabulary
  • Switch between casual and formal conversation modes

Tools used

  • OpenAI GPT
  • Rasa NLU
  • Whisper by OpenAI
  • Text-to-Speech by ElevenLabs
